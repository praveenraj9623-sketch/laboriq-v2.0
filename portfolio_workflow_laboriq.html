LaborIQ Labor Market Intelligence

LaborIQ transforms raw job postings into Lightcast-style workforce intelligence: standardized roles and skills, salary and demand analytics, emerging skill forecasts, statistical validation, and a Streamlit dashboard for stakeholder decisions.

Input

1,590 processed postings from India 2025 data, sample CSVs, and optional Adzuna API pulls.

Core

Cleaned job records enriched with source labels, skills, role families, salary fields, and monthly demand tables.

Models/Methods

Taxonomy NLP, keyword plus TF-IDF role mapping, DuckDB SQL, Logistic Regression, Ridge, and SciPy.

Output

Streamlit dashboard, CSV intelligence reports, joblib models, forecasts, validation tables, and skill-gap guidance.

Final Business Value

The project turns job-posting noise into decisions a workforce, talent, or analytics team can explain.
